Scientific methodology

How labs are listed and benchmarked

1. Inclusion logic

Labs are included when they have a plausible role in CBRN defense, national security, public health preparedness, item test and calibration, reference measurement, diagnostics, high-containment research, radiological/nuclear safety, or CBRN-focused R&D.

2. Evidence hierarchy

Priority is given to official institutional sources, government pages, accreditation references, public technical reports, peer-reviewed publications, and recognized international program participation. Each lab has a validation tier and confidence level.

3. Weighted benchmark model

The v2 score is calculated from seven criteria: strategic relevance, technical capability, operational support, accreditation/standards, research output, infrastructure maturity, and international collaboration.

4. Expert interpretation

Scores are decision-support indicators, not absolute rankings. Expert review should consider mission similarity, public-source visibility, classified capability gaps, and relevance to national CBRN early warning and WRS ecosystems.
